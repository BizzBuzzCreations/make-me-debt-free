Our Services

Debt Help & Financial Support Services Across the UK

We support individuals across the UK who are looking for debt help, repayment options, and financial guidance tailored to their situation. Whether your debt comes from credit cards, personal loans, or everyday expenses, we help you understand structured solutions that may be available to you.

Most Common

IVA (Individual Voluntary Arrangement)

A formal, legally binding agreement that helps you repay affordable monthly payments over a fixed period.

  • Legally agreed repayment plan with creditors
  • Fixed monthly payments based on affordability
  • Protects you from creditor action during the arrangement
  • Remaining eligible debt may be cleared after completion (subject to terms)
Best for: People with unsecured debts who need structured long-term support.
Learn More

Debt Management Plan (DMP)

An informal arrangement that helps you repay debts through reduced, manageable monthly payments.

  • One affordable monthly payment
  • Creditors may freeze interest (not guaranteed)
  • Flexible and can be adjusted if your situation changes
  • No legal commitment required
Best for: People who can repay debts over time but need reduced pressure.
Learn More

Bankruptcy

A legal process designed for individuals who are unable to repay their debts.

  • Formal insolvency process in the UK
  • Can help clear certain debts (subject to rules)
  • May affect assets and credit rating
  • Typically considered as a last-resort option
Best for: People with severe financial difficulty who cannot repay debts.
Learn More

Debt Relief Order (DRO)

A low-cost insolvency solution for individuals with low income, limited assets, and small debt levels.

  • Freezes eligible debts for 12 months
  • Debts may be written off if situation does not improve
  • Designed for low-income individuals
  • Strict eligibility criteria apply
Best for: People with low income and minimal assets.
Learn More
Scotland

Protected Trust Deed

A formal agreement available in Scotland to help manage unsecured debts through affordable payments.

  • Legally binding arrangement with creditors
  • Monthly payments based on affordability
  • Protects from creditor legal action
  • Remaining debt may be written off after completion (subject to terms)
Best for: Residents in Scotland with unsecured debts.
Learn More
Scotland

Sequestration

The Scottish legal form of bankruptcy for individuals unable to repay their debts.

  • Formal insolvency process in Scotland
  • May write off qualifying debts (subject to rules)
  • Can affect assets and credit rating
  • Usually considered as a last-resort option
Best for: Severe financial hardship cases in Scotland.
