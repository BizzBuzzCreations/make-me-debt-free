Section 01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide useful information to website owners.

Cookies do not contain any information that personally identifies you by default, but personal information that we store about you may be linked to information stored in and obtained from cookies.
Section 02

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. Specifically, we use them to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website and which pages are most popular
  • Improve website performance and overall user experience
  • Remember user preferences so you don't have to re-enter information
  • Analyse website traffic and help us make informed decisions about content
Section 03

Types of Cookies We Use

We use three categories of cookies on our website. Here is a breakdown of each type and its purpose:

Required

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality. The site cannot work properly without these cookies.

Optional

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand visitor behaviour so we can improve content and page performance.

Functional

Functionality Cookies

Improve your user experience by remembering your preferences and settings.

Section 04

Managing Cookies

You have full control over cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to accept, decline, or delete cookies at any time. Here is how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

C
Chrome → Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
F
Firefox → Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
S
Safari → Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
E
Edge → Settings → Cookies & Site Permissions
Please note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the site to operate correctly.
Section 05

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website may be placed by third-party services such as analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics) or embedded content providers. These third-party cookies are governed by the respective providers' own privacy and cookie policies.

We only work with trusted third-party providers and do not permit them to use your data for their own marketing or advertising purposes. You can opt out of third-party analytics tracking directly through your browser settings or through each provider's opt-out mechanism.

Section 06

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, new cookie technologies, or updates to our website functionality.

When we make significant changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
