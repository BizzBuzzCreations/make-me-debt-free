Top 10 Debt Solution Companies in the UK (2026 Guide)

20 July 2026

Managing debt can be one of the most stressful financial challenges a person can experience. Rising living costs, unexpected expenses, and changing financial circumstances can make it difficult for many people in the UK to keep up with repayments.

The good news is that debt problems can be managed with the right information and support. Many organisations and debt solution providers in the UK help individuals understand their financial situation, explore available options, and choose a suitable path towards becoming debt free.

However, finding the right debt solution company can be confusing. Some organisations provide free debt advice, while others specialise in specific solutions such as Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVAs), Debt Management Plans (DMPs), Debt Relief Orders (DROs), and other financial arrangements.

This guide explores the Top 10 Debt Solution Companies in the UK in 2026, including debt advice organisations, debt guidance platforms, and specialist providers.

Note: This article is for information purposes only. Every financial situation is different. We strongly recommend seeking qualified advice before making any decisions about a debt solution.

What Are Debt Solution Companies?

Debt solution companies and organisations help individuals understand and manage their financial difficulties. They may provide:

  • Debt advice and guidance
  • Budget planning support
  • Debt Management Plans (DMPs)
  • Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA) information
  • Debt Relief Order guidance
  • Bankruptcy information
  • Support with understanding creditor communication

Before choosing a provider, it is important to research different options and understand which solution may be suitable for your circumstances.

Top 10 Debt Solution Companies in the UK (2026)

1

Step Change Debt Charity

Best for: Free debt advice and financial support

StepChange is one of the UK's leading debt advice organisations, helping thousands of people understand and manage their financial difficulties. The organisation provides free support for individuals dealing with different types of debt, including credit cards, loans, and household debts.

Services include:

  • Free debt advice
  • Budget planning
  • Debt Management Plans
  • Financial guidance
  • Support with repayment options

StepChange is often chosen by people who need independent advice before deciding which debt solution may work best for their circumstances.

2

Debt Free Path

Best for: UK debt guidance and understanding debt solutions

Debt Free Path is a UK-focused debt guidance platform designed to help individuals understand different debt solutions and improve their financial knowledge. The platform provides information about:

  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVA)
  • Debt Management Plans (DMP)
  • Bankruptcy options
  • Debt Relief Orders (DRO)
  • Debt management strategies

Debt Free Path focuses on making complicated debt topics easier to understand by providing clear information about possible solutions.

3

National Debtline

Best for: Free debt information and practical guidance

National Debtline provides free and confidential debt advice to individuals across the UK. Services include:

  • Free and confidential debt advice
  • Understanding rights regarding debt
  • Guidance on dealing with creditors
  • Information on available debt solutions
  • Support with managing household finances
4

PayPlan

Best for: Debt management solutions

PayPlan is a UK debt advice provider that helps individuals manage their debts through structured repayment options. Services include:

  • Debt Management Plans (DMP)
  • Free debt advice and assessment
  • Financial situation review
  • Repayment planning and support
  • Creditor negotiation assistance
5

Citizens Advice

Best for: General financial guidance

Citizens Advice provides support on a wide range of issues, including debt and money problems. Services include:

  • Free debt guidance and information
  • Budgeting and money management advice
  • Consumer rights support
  • Help understanding debt options
  • Signposting to specialist debt services
6

Christians Against Poverty (CAP)

Best for: Personal debt coaching and support

Christians Against Poverty (CAP) provides debt support through local centres across the UK. Services include:

  • One-to-one debt coaching
  • Budget planning support
  • Financial education and skills
  • Practical financial guidance
  • Community-based support network
7

Make Me Debt Free

Best for: Personalised debt solution guidance

MakeMeDebtFree helps individuals understand different debt solutions and explore possible options based on their personal financial circumstances. The platform provides guidance around:

  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVA)
  • Debt Management Plans (DMP)
  • Debt solution options
  • Steps towards becoming debt free

MakeMeDebtFree aims to simplify the process by helping people understand their options and make informed decisions about their financial future.

8

IVA Specialists

Best for: Individuals considering an IVA

IVA specialists help individuals understand whether an Individual Voluntary Arrangement may be suitable for their circumstances. Services include:

  • IVA eligibility assessment
  • Explanation of repayment commitments
  • Guidance on impact on credit history

9

Debt Management Providers

Best for: Managing multiple unsecured debts

Debt management providers help individuals organise repayments into a more manageable structure. Services include:

  • Debt consolidation into one monthly payment
  • Credit card and personal loan debt support
  • Overdraft and unsecured borrowing management
  • Affordable repayment plan setup
  • Communication with creditors on your behalf

Bankruptcy Advice Specialists

Best for: People facing serious financial difficulties

Bankruptcy advice specialists help individuals understand whether bankruptcy could be an appropriate solution. Services include:

  • Bankruptcy eligibility guidance
  • Explanation of costs and process
  • Information on impact on assets and property
  • Guidance on alternatives to bankruptcy
  • Support understanding long-term financial consequences

How to Choose the Right Debt Solution Company?

Choosing the right provider requires careful consideration.

  1. 1 Understand Your Financial Situation — Before choosing a solution, understand your total debt amount, monthly income, essential expenses, and current repayments.
  2. 2 Compare Different Solutions — Different debt solutions work differently. Compare options such as IVA, DMP, DRO, and Bankruptcy to find which may suit your circumstances.
  3. 3 Check Transparency — A reliable provider should clearly explain fees, risks, benefits, and possible outcomes before you commit to anything.
  4. 4 Look for Suitable Support — Choose a provider that understands your circumstances and provides information that helps you make an informed decision rather than pressuring you towards a specific product.

Frequently Asked Questions

The best debt solution company depends on your individual circumstances. Different organisations provide different types of support, including free advice, debt management, and formal debt solutions.

Some debt solutions may allow individuals to repay less than the full amount owed depending on their circumstances and creditor agreements.

Some organisations such as StepChange, National Debtline, and Citizens Advice provide free debt advice. However, some providers charge fees for specific services or formal debt solutions. Always ask about fees before proceeding.

An IVA is a formal legal agreement with creditors, usually lasting five to six years, after which remaining eligible debt may be written off. A Debt Management Plan focuses on managing repayments over time without legal enforcement.

Start by reviewing your debts, creating a realistic budget, and understanding the available solutions. Then seek appropriate guidance from a qualified debt specialist. A free consultation can help clarify which options are available for your specific situation.

Conclusion

Finding the right debt solution company can be an important step towards improving your financial future. Whether you need free advice, repayment support, or information about formal debt solutions, understanding your options can help you make better financial decisions.

MakeMeDebtFree provides guidance and resources to help individuals understand debt solutions and explore possible routes towards becoming debt free.

Remember, there is no single solution that works for everyone. Taking time to understand your financial situation and exploring suitable options can help you move towards a more secure future.
